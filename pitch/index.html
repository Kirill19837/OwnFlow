How It Works

Task to PR in three steps

1

Assign & Refine

Assign an AI actor. It reads the project context, asks the right questions, and captures every decision into structured fields automatically.

2

AI Declares Ready

When the AI has everything it needs, it signals readiness. An amber indicator appears. You review and approve with one click — or push back.

3

Execute & Ship

The AI implements, commits to a new branch, and opens a pull request on GitHub — all linked back to the task automatically.
